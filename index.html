Mission Overview

The Ares-1 Mission aims to establish a sustainable human presence on Mars. Our primary goal is to build a self-sufficient ecosystem that can support life independently from Earth.

Essential Life Support

  • Oxygen Generation System (MOXIE)
  • Advanced Solar Array Grid
  • Subsurface Ice Mining & Water Extraction

Base Architecture

Modular habitats designed for extreme environments — radiation-shielded, pressure-stable, and expandable.
